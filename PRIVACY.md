# Privacy Policy

## GitlabClip

**Last updated:** 2026-03-25

### Data Collection

GitlabClip does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.

### How It Works

- The extension reads the page title and URL of GitLab Issue and Merge Request pages **locally** in your browser.
- When you click the copy button, the Markdown-formatted link is copied to your clipboard.
- No data is sent to any server or third party.

### Permissions

The extension requests access to all websites solely to support self-hosted GitLab instances with varying domain names. The extension only activates on pages matching GitLab's URL patterns (`/-/issues/` and `/-/merge_requests/`) and takes no action on other websites.
